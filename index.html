Walking Together

Your journey, step by step.

At Wisdom Surgery Clinic, your care follows a considered and transparent process. Here's what you can expect.

1

Consultation & Understanding

We begin by listening. Your concerns, medical history, expectations and goals are explored in detail. Clinical examination and appropriate imaging guide the discussion.

2

Collaborative Planning

Treatment options are explained clearly, including risks, benefits, alternatives, likely recovery, and costs. You are supported to make an informed decision without pressure.

3

Preparation

Surgical planning is meticulous and collaborative. We engage with other members of your healthcare team when formulating your surgical plan. Pre-operative instructions are provided clearly and tailored to your procedure and health profile.

4

Surgery

Procedures are performed with precision and discipline, in carefully selected facilities aligned with safety and quality standards.

5

Recovery & Follow-Up

Post-operative care is structured and proactive. We monitor healing, address concerns early, and remain accessible — including a direct number to reach your surgeon.

6

Long-Term Outcome

Where appropriate, we coordinate ongoing care with your referring practitioner and allied health providers after the conclusion of surgery and recovery.

It's an honour to be a partner in your journey.

What We Treat

Our services

Wisdom Teeth

Simple, surgical, and complex extractions — including fully impacted teeth. Dr Raymond has specialist expertise in managing teeth in close proximity to the sinuses and nerves, where precision and experience matter most.

Dental Implants

A tooth replacement option designed to look, feel, and function like natural teeth — single tooth, multiple teeth, or full-arch solutions placed with precision.

Bone Grafting

Bone grafting and sinus lifts to restore jaw volume lost to tooth extraction, infection, or injury — rebuilding the foundation needed for successful implant placement.

Exposure of Teeth

Surgical exposure of unerupted or impacted teeth to support orthodontic treatment and help teeth find their correct position.

Orthognathic Jaw Surgery

Surgical correction of jaw and facial skeletal discrepancies to improve function, bite alignment, and facial balance — closely coordinated with your orthodontist.

Soft Tissue Surgery

Biopsies, frenectomies, mucoceles, and other soft tissue procedures performed with care and precision.

Jaw Cysts & Bone Lesions

Diagnosis and surgical management of cysts, benign tumours, and other lesions affecting bones of the jaw and face — with thorough pathology review and careful follow-up.

Pre-Prosthetic Surgery

Preparing the mouth for dentures or other prosthetic work with ridge smoothing, shaping, and optimisation of the supporting tissues.
